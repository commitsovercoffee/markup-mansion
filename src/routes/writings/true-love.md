You don't find **the one**. You arrive there.

Many people claim they have found "the one". Ofcourse they do, cause if you're
with someone whatelse will you say ? That they are not "the one" ? Sounds counterproductive.

This blog is probably for youngers folks, who fall in love everyday ...

There are literally over a billion people in the world. So, how do we find the one ?
Let's start making the world smaller step by step.

1. Who's even in the running for this?

- Someone who I find attractive i.e good personality, pinch of humor ability to take sarcasm.
- Someone I can talk to.
- Someone with whom I can enjoy life.
